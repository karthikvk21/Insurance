Disruption in the Insurance Industry

The insurance industry has been significantly impacted by technology in recent years. Technological advancements have transformed the way insurers operate, from underwriting and claims management to customer engagement and marketing. Technologies like AI / ML, Advanced Analytics, IoT, Cloud Computing, Blockchain and Automation are transforming the insurance industry by optimizing, streamlining and automating processes while generating insights and data which are enabling insurers to develop new and innovative solutions and reach customers more effectively.
